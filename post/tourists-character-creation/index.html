N-Dimensional Tourists Character Creation
Written by David Teller with ♥ on October 29, 2018 in rpg improv space-time-deck plotonomicon n-dimensional-tourists

Past, Present and Quest
  • Pick or draw one card each.

Attribute

If you don’t know in which order to put them:

  • draw cards until you get an Attribute. It is your strongest Attribute. Use the draw to describe how you typically use it;

  • draw cards until you get a second Attribute. It is your second strongest Attribute. Use the draw…

  • …

  • Use the deck to determine past, present, future.

  • Use the deck to order and/or give flavour to your attributes.
