N-Dimensional Tourists: Conflicts, Countdowns and other Races
Written by David Teller with ♥ on November 4, 2018 in rpg plotonomicon n-dimensional-tourists

Previously, we have seen how to setup and meet Challenges in N-Dimensional Tourists. Throughout a game of N-Dimensional Tourists, you are going to use this rule in all sorts of situations.

In this chapter, we are going to build upon this rule and discuss how to setup particular kinds of Challenges, such as conflicts, countdowns, races, boss fights or horror situations.

Setting up and resolving conflicts

Let us insist upon one point first: by opposition to most Role-Playing Games, N-Dimensional Tourists does not have special rules for violence. In fact, in the chapter dedicated to Challenges, we have already seen two examples that could be qualified as conflicts:

  • The Chess Match, an example of intellectual conflict;
  • The Kaiju Attack, an example of physical conflict.
