Space·Time Deck: The Suit of Out There
Written by David Teller with ♥ on October 18, 2018 in rpg improv space-time-deck plotonomicon

Well, after the Triumphs and four suits (Paper, Rock, Blade, Serpent), it is time to conclude the Space·Time Deck with its last suit: the Suit of Out There (also known as Suit of Spock)!

Out There symbolizes:

  • imagination;
  • dream
  • confusing dream and reality.

The cards:

  • Ruler
  • Dragon
  • Treasure
  • Soldier
  • Builder
  • Seeker
  • Lover
  • Servant
  • Home
  • Foot (blank, customizable)
  • Teddy bear (blank, customizable)
