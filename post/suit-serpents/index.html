Space·Time Deck: The Suit of Serpents
Written by David Teller with ♥ on October 17, 2018 in rpg improv space-time-deck plotonomicon

Did you think the Space·Time Deck was done after

  • the Triumphs;
  • the Suit of Paper;
  • the Suit of Rock;
  • the Suit of Blade?

Were it only that it were so simple!

Here’s the fourth suit: the Suit of Serpents.

Serpents traditionally symbolize:

  • speed;
  • stealth;
  • adaptation;
  • survival;
  • lies;
  • forbidden knowledge;
  • forbidden power;
  • temptation.

The cards:

  • Ruler
  • Dragon
  • Treasure
  • Soldier
  • Builder
  • Seeker
  • Lover
  • Servant
  • Home
  • Foot (blank, customizable)
  • Teddy bear (blank, customizable)
